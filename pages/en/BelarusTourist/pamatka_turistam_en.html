Travel advisory when contacting travel agencies.

In regard with the increasing number of dishonest obligation cases along with individual tourist agencies committing unlawful acts against tourists, the Tourism Department of the Sports and Tourism Ministry of the Republic of Belarus (hereinafter referred to as the Tourism Department) recommends finding out some relevant information on a travel company in public sources foremost for determining the existence of this company before tour purchasing or ordering services. These data may be obtained on the official website of the Legal Entities and Individual Entrepreneurs Unified State Register.

In addition to the tourist company's existence information, it is also advisable to obtain information about the data available in the Register of tourist entities, the formation, and maintenance of which is carried out by the Tourism Department. This resource is open, and you can find it by the following link: mst.by/ru/tourists-belarus-ru.

The information above, as well as the necessary legal advice, can be obtained by contacting the Tourism Department by phone: 8 017 327 23 25 (Tourism Department Chief Council Alexander Igorevich Krechetov).

When contacting a travel agency, the Tourism Department recommends you to read thoroughly the contract terms before purchasing services. Please note that the contract for the travel services provision is drawn up in accordance with the standard form established by law and must not contain terms contradicting the law. For other types of services, the standard contract form is not provided.

Regardless of whether a tour is purchased or individual services are ordered (booking, consulting, visa support), a printed contract for the provision of services is required. The cases related to transportation and (or) accommodation, where confirmation of this agreement is considered to be a ticket or a document to check into the accommodation facility (voucher, etc.) are exceptions. However, if transportation and (or) accommodation services are purchased in a package tour along with other services that are not related to transportation or accommodation, it is necessary to draw up a tourist services agreement with the subsequent documents for transportation and (or) accommodation issuing.

Please note that the contract should be signed upon all its essential terms agreement. Those are the terms of the subject matter, the named legislative terms, necessary or mandatory for contracts of this type.

Before signing an agreement on the tourist services provision, the presence of the following sections should be checked: the cost of services, the time and procedure for their payment, the rights and obligations of the parties, information about the contractor, customer and third parties for whom the services are purchased, a tourist travel program.

After signing up the contract, payment must be made in the accordance with the contract, the payment verification must be confirmed by the issuing cash receipt or other payment documents herewith the tourist's demand for the issuance of a payment document directly follows the Law of the Republic of Belarus " the Protection of Consumer's Rights Act".

Given the fact that many travel companies provide tourists with visa support, we draw attention to the fact that, within the meaning of The provision of tourist services Rules, the contractor upon receiving the required documents for the visa (visas) obtaining to the country (place) of temporary residence (transit travel), if necessary, consults on issues of paperwork, as well as warns about reasons that may result in refusal to open a visa(s).

The contractor has the right to refuse to accept documents from a tourist in the case of a reasonable doubt about the filling correctness with the exception of a written guarantee of the correctness of the documents submitted personally or by submitting confirmation.

We also note that the original documents are necessary for the tourist services provision, as well as the currency for payment of the fee when providing visa support, are transferred to the contractor in accordance with the transmitting regulations which is drawn up in two copies. One copy remains with the contractor, the other is given to the tourist.
