2.The grounds for visa-free entry into the Republic of Belarus, departure from the Republic of Belarus, temporary stay in the territory of the Republic of Belarus and transit through the territory of the Russian Federation are:

- for the persons listed in the memo paragraph 1.1 - valid documents for travelling abroad, inclusion in the list of the accreditation system of the European games and the accreditation card;

- for foreign tourists named in memo paragraph 1.2 - valid documents for traveling abroad and an authentic or electronic ticket for the European games sports events and (or) their opening and (or) closing ceremonies. Children under 7 years old can enter the Games free of charge without a seat. However, for visa-free entry to the territory of the Republic of Belarus, you must purchase a ticket to the Games, including children under 7 years old.

3. The terms for issuing a valid visa for the period from June 10 to July 10, 2019 for foreign tourists are not presented in points 1.1 and 1.2 of the memo, going to sports events within the European games and (or) their opening and (or) closing ceremonies, is the authentic or electronic ticket for such events.

The visitors who require a visa should contact the Embassy or Consulate of Belarus to apply for. To obtain a visa, you must submit the following documents for consideration:

∑passport;

∑visa application form;

∑photo;

∑authentic l or electronic ticket;

∑medical insurance the amount of at least 10,000 euros, valid in Belarus;

* payment of the consular fee.

The visa application form, as well as information about the procedure for submitting documents and paying the consular fee, should be obtained by going to the website of the specific Embassy or Consulate.

Contact information of diplomatic missions and Consular offices of Belarus abroad is available at: http://belarusfacts.by/belembassy/.

For persons who are to participate in sports events with a visa to enter the Republic of Belarus and persons transit through the Russian Federation, a Russian transit visa (single or double) and an authentic (electronic) ticket for events are required.

∑        the period of stay in Belarus will not exceed 30 calendar days (including days of arrival and departure);

∑        arrival in Belarus and subsequent departure will be carried out via Minsk national airport on international flights (all flights, except those arriving from Russia or departing to Russia);

∑        a regular passport will be used for the trip, since this travel regime does not apply to diplomatic, service, special and other equivalent passports.

∑        money: at least 25 euros (or the equivalent amount in US dollars or Belarusian rubles) for each day of stay;

To take part in the events of the European games as fans, tickets can be purchased directly in the Republic of Belarus.

When entering the Republic of Belarus, foreign citizens must state exactly the purpose of entry and the chosen staying conditions to the border guards.

5. A ticket for a sporting event entitles you to enter the Republic of Belarus once, including transit through the Russian Federation. Entry must be made no later than the day of the event (or on the day) specified in the ticket. Departure from the Republic of Belarus, including transit through the Russian Federation, is optional yet no later than July 10, 2019.

6.During the preparation and holding of the European games (May 20-July 10, 2019) there will be an Agreement between the Government of the Republic of Belarus and the Government of the Russian Federation on certain issues related to the entry of foreign citizens and stateless persons to international sports events dated May 29, 2018, which regulates travel to the Republic of Belarus through the territory of the Russian Federation.

Foreign citizens and stateless persons specified in paragraph 1.1 of the memo enter, leave, stay and transit through the territory of the Russian Federation from May 20 to July 10, 2019 without obtaining Russian visas using a valid identity document, provided that they are included in the list of the European Games accreditation system and have an accreditation card.

Foreign tourists specified in paragraph 1.2. in the period from June 10 to July 10, 2019, enter, leave, stay and transit through the territory of the Russian Federation without issuing Russian visas using a valid identity document and an authentic or electronic ticket (provided that when purchasing an electronic ticket, information about the citizenship, identification document number (passport) of its owner is provided).
